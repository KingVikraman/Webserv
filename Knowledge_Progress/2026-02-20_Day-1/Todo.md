## Questions to Answer

Before moving forward, answer these:

### Question 1
**What does `200` mean in `HTTP/1.1 200 OK`?**

<details>
<summary>Hint</summary>
It's a status code. 200 means "success - everything worked"
</details>

---

### Question 2
**What does `Host: google.com` tell the server?**

<details>
<summary>Hint</summary>
The server might host multiple websites. This tells it which one you want.
</details>

---

### Question 3
**Why did we press Enter twice after the `Host:` line?**

<details>
<summary>Hint</summary>
HTTP uses a blank line to separate headers from body. Two enters = one blank line.
</details>
